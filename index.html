Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mechanical and Automation Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Yunjian Xu. Before that, I received B. Eng. degree from the School of Electrical Engineering and Automation, Wuhan University (WHU) in 2016, and served as a research assistant in Center for Grid Power Electronics (CGPES), and Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD) during 2016-2018.

My research interests cover stochastic dynamic programming and deep reinforcement learning. Specifically, I am dedicated to design optimal control policies and intelligence control algorithms for large scale complex systems under uncertainties, including optimal job scheduling, dynamic resource allocation, optimal scheduling and differentiated pricing for large-scale Electric Vehicle (EV) charging, and smart data-driven control for the Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) systems.
